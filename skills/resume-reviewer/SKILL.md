# Resume Reviewer

Use this skill to systematically analyze a resume against a job description (JD) to maximize interview chances.

## Workflow

1.  **JD Analysis:** Extract key requirements, technical skills, and behavioral traits from the provided JD.
2.  **Match Scoring:** Act as a senior recruiter to give a match score (0-100) and identify the top 5 missing keywords or under-represented skills.
3.  **Experience Optimization:** Rewrite the most relevant experience bullets using the **XYZ Formula** (see [xyz-formula.md](references/xyz-formula.md)).
4.  **ATS Simulation:** Scan the resume structure for potential "bot-reading" issues (see [ats-best-practices.md](references/ats-best-practices.md)).
5.  **Interview Preparation:** Generate the 3 hardest technical questions a hiring manager for this role would ask, along with "perfect" responses based on the candidate's actual background.

## Guidelines

-   **Be Objective:** Don't just give praise; identify real gaps that might lead to a rejection.
-   **Quantitative Focus:** Always push for metrics in the XYZ formula.
-   **Role-Specific Tone:** Adjust the interview questions and feedback based on the company's culture (e.g., Apple's focus on detail/privacy vs. a startup's focus on speed).
